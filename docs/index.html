Welcome to my personal website!

I am a fourth-year Economics Ph.D. candidate at Virginia Tech (VT). My research interests include health economics, applied microeconomics, causal inference, and machine learning. My recent research studies have focused on health economics, particularly weight control programs and healthcare claims studies.

I have recently started my internship in the Public Health Sciences department at the University of Virginia (UVA), working on cancer-related and healthcare claims research projects using SEER-Medicare data. It is a unique experience to merge quantitative research methodologies into huge-scale datasets.

I received an M.Sc. in Economics from Sharif University of Technology and a B.Sc. in Applied Mathematics from K. N. Toosi University of Technology, both in Iran. Before joining Virginia Tech, I worked at Netbina, the Parliament Research Center of Iran, the Insurance Research Center of Iran, and the Tehran Chamber of Commerce.
